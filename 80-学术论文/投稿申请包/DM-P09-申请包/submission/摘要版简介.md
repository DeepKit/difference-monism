# DM-P09 摘要版简介

## 中文短简介

本文主张，可复归程序而非抽象效率或抽象自由，应构成危机治理的最低裁决层。文章提出 `纠错能力 / 纠错载体 / 退出兑现度 / 深度伤害` 四项硬字段，并以 `魏玛 / 日裔拘禁 / USA PATRIOT Act / 纽伦堡` 为样本，说明危机压缩若不能被复审、被限制、被拉回，就会反向侵蚀共同体的承诺结构。本文不做制度终局排名，只提供程序门槛、案例快照与结论边界。

## English Short Summary

This article argues that reversible procedure, rather than abstract efficiency or abstract liberty, should form the minimal level of adjudication in crisis governance. It proposes four hard fields, `correction capacity / correction carriers / exit realization / deep harm`, and applies them to `Weimar / Japanese American incarceration / the USA PATRIOT Act / Nuremberg`. The paper does not offer a final institutional ranking; it offers procedural thresholds, case snapshots, and conclusion boundaries.
