# DM-P04 摘要版简介

## 中文短简介

本文主张，意识问题长期僵持，不只因为它困难，更因为其经典问法过强。与其先把物理过程与体验分处两边，再追问两者如何桥接，不如先问：什么样的结构会形成只能从其内部成立的状态区分。文章据此提出最小内部性的四项门槛，并把与 `GNW / IIT / HOT / 强版 illusionism` 的分歧压到同一比较窗口，再以 `DOC/MCS / 麻醉 / 裂脑 / no-report / 盲视` 等边界案例写出更新规则与失败线。

## English Short Summary

This article argues that the hard problem is stalled not only because it is difficult, but because it is framed too strongly. Instead of first separating physical process and experience and then asking how they might be bridged, the paper asks what kind of structure forms states that can exist only from the inside. It proposes four minimal thresholds of internality, contrasts them with `GNW / IIT / HOT / strong illusionism`, and writes boundary cases and explicit failure conditions into the argument.
