# DM-P00 摘要版简介

## 中文短简介

本文不再把“差异”写成逻辑上唯一可能的终极起点，而只捍卫一个更窄的判断：在 `关系 / 过程 / 事件 / 信息 / 约束` 这些常见候选写法中，`差异 + 非任意约束性` 目前仍是较经济、较少偷带现成对象、时点化切片与编码框架的起点组合。文章把起点竞争改写为前提记账问题，并给出最低比较表、更新规则与明确失败线。

## English Short Summary

This article does not treat difference as the uniquely possible ultimate starting point. It defends a narrower claim: among the common candidates of relation, process, event, information, and constraint, the combination of difference plus non-arbitrary constraint remains the more economical formulation at present. The paper rewrites starting-point competition as presupposition accounting and states a minimal comparison table, update rules, and explicit failure conditions.
