# ExecNorm 摘要版简介

## 中文短简介

本文提出“可执行规范主义”，将部分社会契约转化为形式化、可验证的规范介质，尝试把治理逻辑从事后惩戒压向事前预防与过程约束。当前申请包的重点不是宣称已可直接外投，而是先把哲学根基、技术路径与分层治理原则压到同一论文线下，并为唯一主稿裁决做准备。

## English Short Summary

This paper presents Executable Normativism as an attempt to transform part of the social contract into formal, verifiable normative media, thereby shifting governance logic from ex post punishment toward ex ante prevention and process constraint. The current package is not yet a direct submission-ready paper; its main role is to place the philosophical basis, technical pathway, and layered governance principles within a single paper line before a single authoritative manuscript is chosen.
