# LMM 摘要版简介

## 中文短简介

LMM 不是传统营销促转方法，而是一套面向高责任、高噪声、高损耗环境的问题显影与责任承接系统。当前总述稿把 LMM 压缩为问题显影、弱测量、港口承接、责任客观化与止血优先五条一级命题，并同时写明其测量逻辑、适用边界与后续验证路径。

## English Short Summary

LMM is not presented here as a conventional conversion-oriented marketing method, but as a responsibility-bounded intervention framework for high-responsibility, high-noise, and high-loss environments. The current overview compresses the framework into five first-order propositions: problem illumination, weak measurement, port-based intake, objective responsibility, and damage-first handling, while also stating its measurement logic, scope limits, and later validation path.
