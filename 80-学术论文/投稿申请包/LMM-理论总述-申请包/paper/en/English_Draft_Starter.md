# LMM: A Responsibility-Bounded Intervention Framework for High-Noise Intake Environments

**Status**: English draft starter for submission package

## Abstract

LMM is presented not as a conventional marketing conversion method, but as an intervention framework for high-responsibility, high-noise, and high-loss environments. It prioritizes problem illumination, weak measurement, port-based intake, objective responsibility, and damage-first handling. At its current stage, this package should be treated as a theory overview rather than a completed empirical paper.

## Keywords

responsibility-bounded intake; weak measurement; problem illumination; governance; intervention framework
