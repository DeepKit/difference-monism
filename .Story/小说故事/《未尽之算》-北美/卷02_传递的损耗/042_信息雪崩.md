# 第42集：信息雪崩

> **《未尽之算》·第42集·信息雪崩**
> **本卷进度：18/24 | 全书倒计时：966集**

Registrar Zhao’s study was a graveyard of black official boxes.

One hundred. Exactly one hundred.

He had opened ninety-nine of them.

The floor was a mess of stones, dried leaves, scraps of cloth, and sheet after sheet of paper that read: "Has My Lord eaten yet?"

Zhao’s eyes were a web of broken red capillaries. His hands were blistered from the incessant use of the wax-cutter.

"Lunatics..." he whispered to the empty room. "Wang Deren is a madman. Everyone at Juyong Pass has lost their minds."

He reached for the hundredth box.

His hand shook.

What was in this one? Dog manure? A dead rat?

He didn't want to know anymore. He was beyond caring.

"Guards!" Zhao shrieked, his voice raw. "Bring the brazier!"

"My Lord, shall we not open the final one?"

"No! Burn it! Burn it all! Burn this box, and burn every letter that comes from Juyong Pass from this day forward! Burn them all!"

"But My Lord, what if..."

"There is no 'what if'! They’re all fakes! They’re all liars!"

Zhao snatched the hundredth box and hurled it into the flaming brazier.

Flame roared three feet high, a hungry orange tongue licking the air.

In reality, that box contained a personal letter. It was Wang Deren’s final testament, written to his elderly mother in the capital.

But Zhao didn't know. He didn't *want* to know.

To him, every message had become Noise.

And to eliminate the Noise, he chose to eliminate the Source.

**When the volume of information exceeds the limit of human processing, the mind chooses blindness.**
