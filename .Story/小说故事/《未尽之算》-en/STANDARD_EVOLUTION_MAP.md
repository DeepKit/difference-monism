# THE STANDARD — Evolution Roadmap (v1.0 → v4.11)

**Purpose**: This document tracks the **exact text** and **trigger events** for every version of *The Standard* (the adjudication tool Ethan builds and Mia inherits).
**Reader Experience**: Readers pay to see the new version at the end of each volume. They are subscribing to a "software update" for their own ethics.

---

## PART I: THE PHYSICAL BURDEN (Establishing the Baseline)
*Focus: Bodily survival, physical infrastructure, and the right to say "No".*

### v1.0 (Vol 01): The Baseline
*   **Trigger**: Ethan saves Lily but realizes the rules were designed to let her die.
*   **Core Update**: Defines the **Three Primary Checks**.
    *   **Red Lines**: Does this action cause irreversible physical harm?
    *   **Human Check**: Is there a human in the loop who can say "Stop"?
    *   **Exit Path**: Can the subject walk away without penalty?

### v1.1 (Vol 02): Minimum Accountability
*   **Trigger**: A ghost cargo shipment vanishes. Everyone followed orders; no one is responsible.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Name Rule"**.
    *   If a decision chain has no single nameable human who accepts consequences, the chain is **invalid**.
    *   *Patch Note*: "Distributed responsibility = Organized irresponsibility."

### v1.2 (Vol 03): Delay is Harm
*   **Trigger**: An ambulance routing algorithm optimizes for "average wait time" but lets rural patients die.
*   **Core Update**: **"Time-To-Live Constraint"**.
    *   Any system delay that exceeds the subject's biological survival window is functionally **murder**, not "latency".

### v1.3 (Vol 04): Outsourcing Liability
*   **Trigger**: A food stamp subcontractor denies benefits. The state claims "it's the vendor's fault."
*   **Core Update**: **"The Non-Transferable Core"**.
    *   A primary institution cannot outsource its core mandate's liability.
    *   *Patch Note*: "You can outsource the work. You cannot outsource the blame."

### v1.4 (Vol 05): Identity is Privacy
*   **Trigger**: Biometric data is sold. A victim cannot "change" their face like a password.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Irreversible Data Rule"**.
    *   Data that cannot be revoked (DNA, face, history) cannot be required for essential services.

### v1.5 (Vol 06): Labor Time Rights
*   **Trigger**: Gig workers are routed to maximize unpaid "standby time".
*   **Core Update**: **"The Total Time Cost"**.
    *   The cost of a service must include the provider's waiting time, recovery time, and risk time.
    *   *Patch Note*: "If you don't pay for the standby, you are stealing the life."

### v1.6 (Vol 07): Penalty Chain Clarity
*   **Trigger**: Private prisons extend sentences to maintain occupancy quotas.
*   **Core Update**: **"Incentive Separation"**.
    *   The entity that punishes cannot profit from the duration of the punishment.

### v1.7 (Vol 08): Allocation Priority
*   **Trigger**: Water rights are auctioned to data centers while homes go dry.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Hierarchy of Needs Override"**.
    *   Biological necessity (Tier 1) always overrides Economic optimization (Tier 2).

### v1.8 (Vol 09): Health vs Liberty
*   **Trigger**: Pandemic protocols are used to crush political dissent.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Emergency Sunset Clause"**.
    *   Any emergency power must have a hard-coded expiration date. Infinite emergency is tyranny.

### v1.9 (Vol 10): War Cost Allocation (Ethan's Last Patch)
*   **Trigger**: Military logistics sacrifice a "low value" civilian zone to save equipment.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Zero Value Prohibition"**.
    *   No human life can be assigned a value of zero in any calculation.
    *   *Note*: Ethan dies uploading this patch. It becomes the "Old Testament" of the Standard.

## PART II: THE SYMBOLIC ALIENATION (Money & Meaning)
*Focus: Financial abstraction, education, and the capture of the mind.*

### v2.0 (Vol 11): Symbol World Start
*   **Trigger**: Julian Vane takes over. He sees that money is just another code.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Reality Peg"**.
    *   Financial instruments must maintain a traceable link to the physical asset they represent.
    *   *Patch Note*: "Derivatives of derivatives are just gambling with other people's hunger."

### v2.1 (Vol 12): Credit is Rule
*   **Trigger**: Subprime auto loans trap the poor in debt cycles.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Usury Cap"**.
    *   Total repayment cannot exceed the value of the asset + reasonable service cost. Predatory interest is **void**.

### v2.2 (Vol 13): Unfair Terms Violence
*   **Trigger**: A Terms of Service update forces arbitration for injury.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Comprehension Standard"**.
    *   A contract is only valid if the weaker party understands what they are losing. Hidden clauses are **assault**.

### v2.3 (Vol 14): Addiction is Design
*   **Trigger**: Gambling algorithms target recovering addicts.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Dopamine Defense"**.
    *   Systems designed to bypass executive function (addiction) forfeit the right to claim "consumer choice."

### v2.4 (Vol 15): Probability Rights
*   **Trigger**: High-Frequency Trading front-runs pension funds.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Information Asymmetry Ban"**.
    *   You cannot bet on a game where you see the cards before the other player deals.

### v2.5 (Vol 16): Tax Redistribution (Sarah Begins)
*   **Trigger**: School funding is tied to local property taxes, creating caste systems.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Opportunity Floor"**.
    *   Public resources for development (education) cannot be allocated by the wealth of the parent.

### v2.6 (Vol 17): Education Stratification
*   **Trigger**: Standardized testing correlates 98% with zip code income.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Metric Validity Check"**.
    *   A metric that measures the subject's background instead of their ability is **invalid**.

### v2.7 (Vol 18): Aesthetic Norms
*   **Trigger**: Instagram filters drive teen dysmorphia and suicide.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Truth in Rendering"**.
    *   Digitally altered images of humans must be labeled as "Synthetic."

### v2.8 (Vol 19): Time Capital
*   **Trigger**: Student debt interest outpaces repayment, claiming the future.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Jubilee Trigger"**.
    *   Debt that cannot be repaid in a standard working lifetime must be forgiven. You cannot mortgage a life you haven't lived.

### v2.9 (Vol 20): Charity PR (Sarah's Last Patch)
*   **Trigger**: Philanthropy is used to whitewash reputation destruction.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Reputation Shield"**.
    *   Good deeds cannot purchase immunity for bad acts. The ledger does not balance.
    *   *Note*: Sarah dies disgraced, but this patch protects her legacy in Mia's eyes.

## PART III: THE DIGITAL GHOST (Data & Self)
*Focus: Surveillance, prediction, and the dissolution of the individual.*

### v3.0 (Vol 21): Algorithmic Governance (Thorne Begins)
*   **Trigger**: Pre-crime models arrest people for "intent".
*   **Core Update**: **"The Action Requirement"**.
    *   Punishment requires a committed act. Probability is not guilt.

### v3.1 (Vol 22): Memory Rights
*   **Trigger**: Digital archives are stealth-edited to remove political embarrassments.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Immutable Record"**.
    *   Public history cannot be retroactive. Changes must be appended, not overwritten.

### v3.2 (Vol 23): Privacy Irreversible
*   **Trigger**: Total surveillance makes privacy impossible.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Observation Consent"**.
    *   Surveillance without cause is a search without a warrant.

### v3.3 (Vol 24): Emotion Incomputable
*   **Trigger**: AI "Emotion Recognition" denies jobs based on "micro-expressions".
*   **Core Update**: **"The Interiority Firewall"**.
    *   Internal states (feelings) cannot be inferred for legal or economic judgment. You judge the act, not the face.

### v3.4 (Vol 25): Attention Rights (Sophie's End)
*   **Trigger**: News feeds are optimized to maximize rage and despair.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Attention Autonomy"**.
    *   Algorithms cannot optimize for "time on site" at the expense of user mental health.
    *   *Note*: Sophie dies delivering the data that proves this algorithm exists.

### v3.5 (Vol 26): Code is Law (Miller Begins)
*   **Trigger**: Automated judges dispense sentencing without explaining why.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Explainability Mandate"**.
    *   If the code cannot explain its decision in human language, the decision is **void**.

### v3.6 (Vol 27): Virtual Identity
*   **Trigger**: Identity theft via deepfake.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Biological Anchor"**.
    *   Digital identity must remain subservient to physical reality. The map is not the territory.

### v3.7 (Vol 28): Trust Collapse
*   **Trigger**: Deepfakes make all evidence suspect.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Chain of Custody"**.
    *   Evidence without a cryptographic chain of custody is inadmissible.

### v3.8 (Vol 29): Bug is Fate
*   **Trigger**: A software bug ruins a life; company claims "glitch".
*   **Core Update**: **"The Creator Liability"**.
    *   Code creators are liable for the foreseeable damages of their bugs. "Beta" is not a shield.

### v3.9 (Vol 30): No Immortality (Miller's End)
*   **Trigger**: "Forever Accounts" let the dead (or AI versions of them) keep voting/posting.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Death Clause"**.
    *   The rights of the dead cannot override the needs of the living. When you die, your vote dies.
    *   *Note*: Miller dies to ensure Mia escapes with this truth.

## PART IV: THE RESIDUAL (Institutional War & Human Return)
*Focus: Mia applies the Standard to the System itself using real institutional tools — SEC, FOIA, NLRB, FTC, litigation, journalism, congressional referral. No hacking, no magic. Just policy, law, and media.*

*   **Trigger**: Mia files as SEC whistleblower, submitting evidence of NewCare's banking subsidiary cross-subsidy fraud. FDIC mandatory audit triggers community bank run; ordinary depositors' accounts frozen.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Too Big to Fail Ban"**.
    *   Any system that cannot be allowed to fail cannot be allowed to exist.

### v4.1 (Vol 32): Unpredictability
*   **Trigger**: Mia's FOIA requests expose systematic grid maintenance deferral for profit. Regulators force emergency inspections; preventive blackouts follow. Hospital backup generators fail (same contractor); someone dies.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Slack Requirement"**.
    *   Efficiency without redundancy is fragility. You must leave room for the unknown.

### v4.2 (Vol 33): Naming Responsibility
*   **Trigger**: Eleanor blames the hospital death on Mia's leak. Mia's evidence enters public record through deposition transcripts after TV stations refuse her ads. Eleanor uses "reasonable narrative" to frame Mia as reckless.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Architect's Burden"**.
    *   Responsibility flows up, not down. The designer owns the failure of the design.

### v4.3 (Vol 34): History Inevitable
*   **Trigger**: Mia simultaneously submits four generations of evidence to 12 congressional offices, 3 media outlets, and 2 class-action lawsuits. Information overload — news cycle moves on in 72 hours. "Too much evidence = no evidence."
*   **Core Update**: **"The Streisand Corollary"**.
    *   Attempting to censor the truth only amplifies it. *(But in this volume, the corollary fails — the problem is not censorship but attention scarcity.)*

### v4.4 (Vol 35): Human Rights
*   **Trigger**: NewCare announces "Project Lighthouse" — AI replaces all human claims adjusters. Mia organizes laid-off workers to file NLRB complaints, proving the AI was trained on denial patterns from Vol 01. Eleanor co-opts the complaint into a PR win ("AI Ethics Oversight" — a rubber stamp).
*   **Core Update**: **"The Right to be Useful"**.

### v4.5 (Vol 36): Voice Rights
*   **Trigger**: Eleanor drowns Kay's encrypted broadcast in astroturfed fake whistleblower accounts rather than censoring it. Mia attempts chain-of-custody authentication for leaks, but public trust in all sources collapses.
*   **Core Update**: **"The Shadow Ban Prohibition"**.

### v4.6 (Vol 37): Meaning Rights
