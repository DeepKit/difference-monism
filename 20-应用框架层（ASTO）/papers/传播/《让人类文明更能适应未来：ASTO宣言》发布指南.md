# 《让人类文明更能适应未来：ASTO宣言》发布指南

## 中文摘要

人类文明正面临前所未有的变迁压力——从气候危机到人工智能，从地缘政治重组到价值观分裂。我们需要的不仅是应对单一挑战的方案，而是一套能够帮助文明在任何重大变迁中保持韧性的底层框架。

**属集变迁存在论（ASTO）** 正是这样一个框架。它从"属集"这一核心概念出发，揭示事物如何在变化中保持同一性、又如何在临界点发生质变。ASTO 不预设特定价值立场，而是提供一套分析工具，帮助我们：

- 识别文明系统中的脆弱点与韧性来源
- 理解变迁的动力学机制与临界条件
- 在保持开放性的同时守护不可让渡的底线
- 让人类在技术与社会变革中始终占据主导位置

这不是一份预言书，而是一份行动指南——献给所有希望让人类文明更好地适应未来的思考者与建设者。

## English Abstract

Human civilization is undergoing unprecedented systemic pressures—from climate disruption and artificial intelligence to geopolitical reconfiguration and value fragmentation. These challenges cannot be adequately addressed through isolated solutions alone. What is required is a foundational framework capable of explaining how civilizations preserve coherence, resilience, and agency across profound transitions.

Attribute-Set Transition Ontology (ASTO) is proposed as such a framework. Centered on the concept of the attribute set, ASTO analyzes how entities maintain identity through continuous change, and how qualitative transformations emerge when critical thresholds are crossed. Rather than prescribing normative values or policy outcomes, ASTO offers an ontological and analytical toolkit for examining complex systems in transition.

Specifically, ASTO enables us to:
Identify structural vulnerabilities and sources of resilience within civilizational systems
Analyze the dynamics, thresholds, and irreversibility of systemic transitions
Distinguish negotiable adaptations from non-negotiable boundaries
Preserve human agency amid accelerating technological and social change

This work does not aim to predict the future. Instead, it provides a conceptual framework for understanding and intervening in transitions as they unfold. It is intended for thinkers, researchers, and practitioners seeking to support the long-term adaptability and continuity of human civilization.
